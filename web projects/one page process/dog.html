How to Make Friends with a Dog

Dogs are commonly known as mans best friends. However many people wonder how do you achive that level of friendship, surely you dont just become friends upon first sight. This guide will give you all the basic instructions you will need to assure that, that dog will become your best friend. We will give you all the do's and don'ts that you will need to suceed in not only making that friendship but maintaining it!


Step One:Bring Treats


It is a known fact that dogs love food, anykind. So when you know that you may be having a possible dog date, it would be well advised to stock up on treats. When you are on your dog date it is imoprtant to not give your treats up right away! Make sure first that the dog you are with is engaging with you and listenting to what you are saying. The you can gradually give them treats to thank them for being such a good listener. If done correctly you can assure that that dog will keep coming back for more and you will secure your second dog date! Here is a list of acceptable treats...

  • bacon
  • steak
  • bones
  • chicken
  • your left overs

Step Two: Have Balls Lots of Them


Dogs love to Play fetch and if you keep a bucket of balls on hand they are gaureented to keep on coming back for more. All balls are acceptable and are a sure fire way to have a great time. Depending on the size of the dog you will want to keep back up balls just incase, they tend to wear them out pretty quickly


Step Three:Celebrate


If you have done all of these things correctly you can now offically claim this dog as your best friend. Now is when all the fun begins, get ready for long walks, cuddle sessions on your couch and plenty of play time. Pat yourself on the back you have done a great job.
